The 20 Healthiest Foods to Eat Every Day

Want to up your nutrition game? Start with this handy list of easy-to-eat options.

1. Oatmeal

Recipe Idea: Berry Baked Oatmeal

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 ½ cups almond milk (or any milk of your choice)
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup or honey
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 cup mixed berries (blueberries, raspberries, or strawberries)
  • ¼ cup chopped nuts (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the oats, milk, maple syrup, and cinnamon. Fold in the berries.
  3. Pour the mixture into a greased baking dish and sprinkle nuts on top (if using).
  4. Bake for 25–30 minutes until set and slightly golden on top.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy!

2. Avocado

Recipe Idea: Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 1 slice whole-grain bread
  • ½ ripe avocado
  • ½ teaspoon lime juice
  • A pinch of salt
  • A pinch of red chili flakes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread until golden and crispy.
  2. Mash the avocado with lime juice and salt in a small bowl.
  3. Spread the avocado mixture onto the toast.
  4. Sprinkle with chili flakes for a spicy kick.

3. Walnuts

Recipe Idea: Honey-Paprika Walnuts

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup walnut halves
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • ½ teaspoon smoked paprika
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix honey, smoked paprika, and salt. Add walnuts and toss to coat evenly.
  3. Spread walnuts on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  4. Roast for 8–10 minutes, stirring halfway through, until fragrant and caramelized.
  5. Let cool and enjoy as a snack or salad topping.

4. Mushrooms

Recipe Idea: Miso Mushroom Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sliced mushrooms (button, cremini, or shiitake)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon white miso paste
  • ½ cup vegetable broth or water
  • 2 servings cooked whole-grain pasta
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add mushrooms and sauté until golden and tender.
  2. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Stir in miso paste and vegetable broth, cooking until the sauce slightly thickens.
  4. Toss in the cooked pasta until well-coated.
  5. Garnish with parsley and serve.

5. Greek Yogurt

Recipe Idea: Chimichurri Yogurt Dip

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

    • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

    1 tablespoon tahini

  • A pinch of salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine all ingredients and mix until smooth.
  2. Serve as a dip for veggies, grilled chicken, or pita bread.

6. Eggs

Recipe Idea: Italian Baked Eggs With Tomato Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ¼ cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Whole-grain bread for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Pour marinara sauce into a small oven-safe skillet or baking dish.
  3. Make four small wells in the sauce and crack an egg into each.
  4. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper over the eggs.
  5. Bake for 10–15 minutes, or until egg whites are set but yolks are still runny.
  6. Garnish with basil and serve with bread.

7. Edamame

Recipe Idea: Edamame Hummus

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked edamame
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

    • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

    1 tablespoon tahini

  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a food processor and blend until smooth.
  2. Adjust the consistency with a little water if needed.
  3. Serve with crackers, veggies, or as a sandwich spread.

8. Kiwi

Recipe Idea: Kiwi-Cucumber Relish

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe kiwis, peeled and diced
  • ½ cucumber, diced
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon chopped mint leaves

Instructions:

  1. Combine kiwi, cucumber, honey, lime juice, and mint in a bowl.
  2. Mix well and let sit for 10 minutes.
  3. Serve as a topping for grilled fish, chicken, or tacos.

9. Sweet Potatoes

Recipe Idea: Stuffed Sweet Potatoes

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes
  • 1 cup black beans, cooked or canned (drained and rinsed)
  • ½ avocado, diced
  • ½ cup salsa
  • 1 tablespoon sour cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Prick sweet potatoes with a fork and bake for 40–50 minutes until tender.
  2. Slice the baked sweet potatoes in half and fluff the insides with a fork.
  3. Top with black beans, avocado, salsa, and sour cream.

10. Berry Smoothie Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • ½ banana
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • Toppings: granola, sliced fruits, shredded coconut

Instructions:

  1. Blend berries, banana, and almond milk until smooth.
  2. Pour into a bowl and top with chia seeds, granola, and fruits.

11. Quinoa Salad with Chickpeas

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • ½ cup cooked chickpeas
  • 1 cup chopped cucumber, tomatoes, and bell peppers
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt, pepper, and parsley

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Toss well and chill before serving.

12. Lentil Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 diced carrots
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 4 cups vegetable broth

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onion, garlic, and carrots in olive oil.
  2. Add lentils, turmeric, and broth.
  3. Simmer until lentils are soft, then blend for a creamy texture.

13. Grilled Salmon with Asparagus

Ingredients:

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 bunch asparagus
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Season salmon and asparagus with olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Grill on medium heat for 10 minutes.

14. Spinach and Feta Omelette

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • ¼ cup crumbled feta
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Whisk eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Sauté spinach, add eggs, and cook until set.
  3. Sprinkle feta before folding.

15. Tofu Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup diced tofu
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, peppers)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Garlic and ginger

Instructions:

  1. Sauté tofu until golden.
  2. Add vegetables, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, and sesame oil.

16. Roasted Cauliflower

Ingredients:

  • 1 head cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Toss cauliflower with olive oil and spices.
  2. Roast at 400°F (200°C) for 25 minutes.

17. Whole-Wheat Pasta Primavera

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked whole-wheat pasta
  • 1 cup steamed vegetables (zucchini, cherry tomatoes, broccoli)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

Instructions:

  1. Toss pasta with olive oil, veggies, and seasoning.

18. Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1 grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • ½ avocado
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic dressing

Instructions:

  1. Combine greens, chicken, and avocado.
  2. Drizzle with dressing.

19. Sweet Potato Wedges

Ingredients:

  • 2 sweet potatoes, cut into wedges
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Toss wedges with oil and spices.
  2. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 30 minutes.

20. Broccoli and Cheddar Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • ½ cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup almond milk

Instructions:

  1. Simmer broccoli in broth until tender.
  2. Blend, add milk, and stir in cheese.
