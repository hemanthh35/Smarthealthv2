About Us

SmartHealth is an AI-powered virtual health assistant designed to assist users with early disease prediction, symptom analysis, and general health guidance. By interpreting user-reported symptoms, SmartHealth can suggest possible conditions, identify early warning signs, and provide preventive advice. SmartHealth also offers tailored lifestyle tips on diet, exercise, and stress management to promote wellness, along with insights on over-the-counter medications, including usage and precautions. Equipped with a vast health knowledge base, SmartHealth empowers users to make informed health decisions and take proactive steps for improved well-being.
